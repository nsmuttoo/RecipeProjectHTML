Hamburger

Home

Description

A hamburger, or simply a burger, is a dish consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bun
  • 250g Ground Beef
  • 1 Slice of your favorite Cheese
  • 30g of Mayonaise
  • 30g of Sriracha
  • A touch of butter
  • Any other toppings of your choice!

Steps

  1. Apply the butter to the inside of the bun
  2. Place the buns in the pan on medium heat to toast
  3. Form the beef into a patty
  4. After the buns are toasted place the patty in the pan and flip after 5 minutes
  5. After the flip place the cheese on the top of the patty
  6. Mix the Sriracha and Mayonaise to form one sauce
  7. Apply this sauce to the inside of the bun
  8. When the patty is cooked place it on the bottom bun
  9. Apply all toppings and place top bun
  10. Enjoy!
